The Importance of Pupillary Distance Measurement for Online Glasses


A critical step when purchasing prescription prescription glasses online is accurately providing your pupillary distance (PD). PD measures the distance between the centers of your pupils, ensuring the optical center of your lenses aligns correctly with your eyes. Only a few U.S. states mandate that PD be included on your prescription.


If your prescription does not include this measurement, you'll need to measure it yourself. Fortunately, many online sites, including reputable brands like Smith Optics, provide easy-to-use online tools and printable rulers to help you accurately determine your pupillary distance measurement for online glasses.
