Bifocal Lenses: Distinct Fields for Distance and Reading


For those who require correction for both distance and reading vision, **bifocal lenses** provide two distinct optical powers within one lens. The upper portion corrects **distance vision**, while the lower segment, typically a visible line, is for **reading vision** or close-up tasks. While `bifocal lenses` offer clear separation between prescriptions, the visible line can be noticeable and may take some adjustment. These lenses offer a practical solution for presbyopia, allowing you to seamlessly switch between viewing distances without changing glasses.
