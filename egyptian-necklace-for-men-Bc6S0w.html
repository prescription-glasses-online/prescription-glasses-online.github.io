    The Critical Role of Pupillary Distance (PD)
    When ordering prescription glasses online, your `pupillary distance (PD) measurement` is absolutely crucial. This number indicates the distance between the centers of your pupils and ensures that the optical center of your lenses aligns perfectly with your eyes. An incorrect PD can lead to discomfort, eye strain, and even blurry vision, negating the benefits of even the best `economical eyewear`. Always ensure your prescription includes your `PD measurement`, as it's a non-negotiable for online orders (Source: billypenn.com). Some forward-thinking online retailers, like Zenni, even allow you to sort glasses by frames compatible with specific `PD numbers`, streamlining your search for `affordable online glasses` (Source: businessinsider.com/zenni-prescription-glasses-review).
